About Building India Inc.

Our theme this year is “Re-building India Holistically: From Agriculture to Space”.

Building India Inc. is a podium to highlight the contributions of Corporate India in the Indian economy and to deliberate on challenges faced by our nation in the constant strive of becoming a global leader. The business summit is an endeavour to connect academia, corporate, socio-economic sector and the Government in pursuit of advocating the great Indian growth and deliberating on challenges lying ahead of future managers of the country. Building India lnc. (BII) witnesses active participation from the drivers of change in the corporate and the social world, faculty from leading institutions and students from top B-schools across the country.
