Udyamita: The Entrepreneurship Conclave

Theme: Building India's next unicorn: Challenges and growing trends

In 2013, when Aileen Lee, founder of Cowboy Ventures, coined the term Unicorn to refer to technology startups with valuations over US$1 billion, India had none. Fast forward to 2021 and India is home to a whopping 34 unicorns, and 52 soonicorns with a potential to become unicorns by 2022.

In this panel we will discuss some tales of triumph, some challenges faced and some emerging trends of the startup ecosystem.
